// 06 — If He Pushes Back
The Objection
The Response
"Doesn't the Foundation do this?"
The Foundation is the stewardship arm — they manage the endowment. We are the activation arm. We build the content engine that feeds them. Kenneth Neighbors and Twuanna Ward do incredible work protecting assets. We're building the marketing engine that creates new ones.
"Is the $250k ARR target realistic?"
We moved away from aspirational numbers. 7,500 paying members at tiered dues plus one corporate sponsor pilot gets us there. The math is in the Revenue Model — I'll leave it with you today. It's not aspirational, it's modeled.
"How much of my time does this actually require?"
Advisory only. Quarterly check-ins, one event appearance per year, your name on the platform. We are not asking for a time commitment that conflicts with KTC Ventures. Your voice when it matters — that is the ask.
"Why me specifically — why now?"
Because you built the 30 for 30. Because you founded The Shadow League at the intersection of sports, race, and culture. Because you are a FAMU Magna Cum Laude graduate who already sits on the Foundation Board. We didn't build this role and go looking for someone. We knew who we needed first.
